Why Exercise and Sleep Matter

Strength training helps your body turn extra calories into muscle instead of fat.

Getting enough sleep allows muscles to recover and grow effectively.

Workout and Recovery Basics

Beginner Strength Exercises

  • Squats or leg press
  • Push-ups or bench press
  • Rows or pull-downs

Simple Weekly Routine

  1. Train 2–3 times per week
  2. Increase weights gradually
  3. Rest and eat properly after workouts
